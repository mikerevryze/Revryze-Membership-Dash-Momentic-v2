PART 2 — Goal Planner Section

Add a new section below the Sales Velocity section called Goal Planner. The logic is as follows:

- Member goal is fixed at 250
- CDL goal is fixed at 500
- Blended close rate = total memberships sold / (total Meta leads + current CDLs)
- Total leads still needed to hit 250 members = (250 - current members sold) / blended close rate
- User inputs projected additional CDLs via a slider or number input
- Remaining paid leads needed = total leads still needed - projected additional CDLs
- Additional ad spend needed = remaining paid leads needed x current CPL
- As the user moves the CDL projection up, the required ad spend goes down in real time. As they move it down, ad spend goes up.

Display the following in the Goal Planner section:
- A progress bar showing current CDLs vs the 500 CDL goal
- A progress bar showing current members sold vs the 250 member goal
- A slider for projected additional CDLs with the resulting required ad spend updating live
- A dynamic callout: "If you drive X more CDLs, you need $Y more in ad spend to hit 250 members"

Everything is per location, same as the rest of the dashboard.
